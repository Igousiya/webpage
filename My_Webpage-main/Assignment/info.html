Domestic Shipping Policy

Shipment processing time All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.
If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days.
Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

Returning In-store Purchases

If you purchased your item in one of our stores you have 28 days from your purchase/date of receipt to return the item to us in store in an unworn, unused condition for an exchange or refund.
All refunds will be processed using the original payment method. If you do not have the original credit or debit card, then your refund won't be issued. To receive a full refund or to exchange your items please ensure you do the following:

-Return/exchange within 28 days of receiving your item(s).
-Make sure items are clean, unworn and with their tags attached.
-Have the original receipt or returns note.
We won’t be able to complete your refund if you can’t meet these 3 requirements.
